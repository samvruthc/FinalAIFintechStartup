Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 30, 2026

1. ELIGIBILITY

  • Request within 7 days of subscription start
  • Fewer than 3 reports generated
  • Both conditions must be met for full refund
  • After 7 days: no refunds under any circumstances
  • Annual plans: same 7-day window from activation date

2. HOW TO REQUEST

  • Email support@orionai.com with subject: 'Refund Request — [registered email]'
  • We process within 5-7 business days
  • Refund to original payment method
  • UPI/bank: 5-10 business days. Card: 7-14 business days

3. NON-REFUNDABLE

  • After 7 days
  • More than 3 reports generated
  • Partial month refunds
  • Dissatisfaction with AI analysis or data accuracy
  • Accounts suspended for ToS violations

4. CANCELLATION

  • Cancel anytime from account settings
  • Access continues until end of billing cycle
  • No partial refunds for unused days

5. FAILED PAYMENTS

  • Retry 3 times over 3 days
  • Then downgrade to Free — no data deleted
  • User notified by email each attempt

6. DISPUTES

  • Contact us before initiating a chargeback
  • Chargebacks without contact may result in suspension
  • We respond to disputes within 5 business days
