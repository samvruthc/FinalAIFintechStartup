Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 28, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Orion AI ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. This policy is designed to comply with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000 and the SPDI Rules 2011, as well as general GDPR principles for our international users.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as information generated through your use of the service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and hashed password. We do not store plain-text passwords.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features accessed, and search queries executed on the platform.
  • Portfolio Data: Tickers, quantities, and buy prices entered by you for tracking and analysis.
  • Payment Information: Processed by our secure third-party payment providers. We do NOT store credit/debit card details on our servers.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Network Information: IP address and approximate location data derived from your network.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected data to provide, maintain, and improve the Orion AI experience.

Our Data Commitment:

  • We DO NOT sell user data to third parties. This is a core principle of our service.
  • We do not allow advertisers to target users through our platform.
  • To process transactions and send related information, including receipts and alerts.
  • To send service-related emails, product updates, and administrative notices.
  • To personalize your experience and optimize our AI-driven research tools.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activity.

4. DATA SHARING

We share information only with trusted service providers to facilitate our service:

  • Payment Processors: Such as Stripe or Razorpay, solely for processing your subscription payments.
  • Infrastructure Providers: Cloud hosting services (e.g., AWS, Railway) used to store data and run our applications.
  • Analytics Providers: We use anonymized usage data to understand platform performance.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, or a valid legal process from government authorities.

We do NOT share personal data with data brokers, marketing agencies, or independent third-party advertisers.

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

  • Storage: Your data is stored on secure servers with restricted access.
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS (SSL/TLS) and at rest on our databases.
  • Hashing: We use industry-standard cryptographic hashing for passwords; your actual password is never visible to us.
  • Breach Notification: In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery.

6. COOKIES

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication, session management, and security.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how users interact with the platform. You may opt out of these through your browser settings.
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use cookies for tracking across third-party sites or for serving advertisements.

A cookie consent banner is displayed on your first visit to manage your preferences.

7. USER RIGHTS

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request updates to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data.
  • Portability: Export your portfolio data in a standard format.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, please email support@orionai.com. We will respond to all valid requests within 30 days.

8. DATA RETENTION

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Account Deletion: Upon deletion, your data is kept for a 30-day grace period before permanent erasure.
  • Financial Records: Transaction and payment records are retained for 7 years to comply with statutory tax and audit requirements.
  • Analytics: Fully anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for trend analysis.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Orion AI is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will take immediate steps to delete the account and all associated data.

10. INTERNATIONAL USERS

Orion AI is operated from India. By using the platform, international users consent to the processing and storage of their data in India. We apply the same high standard of data protection to all users regardless of their geographical location.

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through a prominent notice on the platform. Your continued use of the service after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
